The home of the Glynn Academy Remote Control (GA RC) Club on the internet. Here you can find some of our projects - those we have made, those we have found, and those we have purchased - their stories, their progress, and, for some, their destruction. Our goal is to show every last person who comes across our little slice of the internet just what a good time you can have with remote control aircraft. If we do, at any time, manage to pursuade you, feel free to contact us here, or maybe just show up at our next meeting! Barring unforseen circumstances, a broad collection of possibilities which are primarily weather related, we will meet every Friday after school in the Science Building's Tech Workroom. Be sure to check out our calendar too, because on top of our regular meetings, we sometimes finagle a celebration or two out of the year.
