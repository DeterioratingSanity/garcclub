What We Do

The Glynn Academy Remote Control Club, shorted to GA RC for the sake of everybody who has ever had to write, type, think, or say the whole thing out loud, is a tight-knit group of people who enjoy building, flying and, occasionally, crashing, a vast repository of cardboard, foamboard, and foam aircraft. For our members, flying offers at once freedom from earthly constraints, as, through a small, radio controlled medium you escape for a time the ground, and intense mental stimulation. To keep up with one's aircraft as it soars overhead, it is crucial to preserve your perspective. Much like an interpersonal dynamic, the RC pilot must see not just from their position looking up, but also from the perspective of the plane, always conscious of goings-on in the air.

Where We Are

We do what we do because its a joy to be able to share flight with our friends and with our peers. Many of the former are counted among our members, and we of course invite you cordially to join us every Friday afternoon in the Science Building Tech Room, but for those who cannot attend, either for one meeting or several - as well as those for whom life takes up their Friday afternoons, we have an Instagram page with some of the highlights.
